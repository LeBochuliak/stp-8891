Game Description

Karts Battle throws you into an explosive combat racing experience like no other

This isn’t your usual race — this is survival on wheels. Equip your kart with powerful weapons, use drift skills to dodge and attack, and blast your rivals to smithereens. Whether you prefer tactical precision or pure destruction, the arena is yours to conquer.

With every drift, every bullet, and every crash, you'll inch closer to becoming the ultimate champion. Jump into the arena, customize your battle kart, and show them who’s boss!
